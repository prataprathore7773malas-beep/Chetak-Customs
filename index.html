About Chetak Customs

Chetak Customs is one of Rajasthan's leading custom vehicle workshops, specializing in premium Jeep modifications, Willys restorations, Mahindra Thar customization, motorcycle customization, and heavy-duty fabrication.

Founded with a passion for craftsmanship and engineering excellence, we transform ordinary vehicles into extraordinary machines built for adventure, performance, and timeless style.
