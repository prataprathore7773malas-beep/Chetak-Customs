Where Heritage Meets Horsepower

Every legendary Jeep begins with a vision. At Chetak Customs, we don't simply modify vehicles—we engineer machines that combine timeless heritage with modern performance.

From complete Willys restorations to aggressive off-road builds and luxury Mahindra Thar customization, every project reflects our passion for quality, durability and innovation.
